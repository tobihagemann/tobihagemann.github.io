Last updated: May 30, 2026

This privacy policy applies to apps and games published by Tobias Hagemann.

My apps do not use advertising, analytics, tracking, or third-party tracking SDKs. I do not operate a server backend for these apps and I do not collect or store personal data from the apps on my own systems.

## Game Center

Some games may use Apple Game Center for leaderboards and achievements. If you sign in to Game Center and use these features, the app may send your scores and achievement progress to Apple so Game Center can provide those features. This gameplay information is associated with your Game Center account and is handled by Apple according to Apple's privacy policies.

You can manage Game Center in your device settings.

## Local Data

Apps may store preferences locally on your device, such as gameplay settings, tutorial state, or theme choices. This data stays on your device and is not sent to me.
