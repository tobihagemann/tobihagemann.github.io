Cryptomator

Cryptomator provides transparent, client-side encryption for your cloud. Protect your documents from unauthorized access. Cryptomator is free and open source software, so you can rest assured there are no backdoors.

cryptomator.org

Spectrum Run

Spectrum Run is all about the three colors Red, Blue and Yellow - packed in a fast-paced action-arcade game! Jump, dive and slide through gates with the right color in a challenging hunt for the Highscore! Master your reaction-time while Doc White is gaining speed. Earn Achievements and challenge your friends on Game Center!

tooPassword

tooPassword for iOS offers read-only(!) access to AgileBits' 1Password keychains. The project has been discontinued, that's why it's now fully open source. You can still download it for free on the App Store.

setoLabs/tooPassword on GitHub

bonntermin

Automatically scrape Stadt Bonn's Dienstleistungszentrum appointment service for the earliest month that you can reserve. If this bookable month is the one you desire, you'll receive an email notification.
